Overview

I am an IT undergraduate from RMIT University Vietnam - SGS Campus is currently developing a fitness app with a view to increasing people’s healthy lifestyles and also raising people’s awareness of exercising regularly. I am looking for potential teammates in order to advance the application.

Job scope: You will have opportunities to contribute to the development process of a practical application. You may design the visuals of the app (such as images, videos, …), develop functions for the app by coding (code a search bar, add sections related to healthcare, …). Moreover, you are responsible for maintaining the app and monitoring the operation of our product, as well as update patch and fix bugs whenever needed.

Job requirements and qualifications: You are ought to be familiar with basic coding skills and have knowledge in some coding languages (Python, JavaScript are preferential). In addition, majoring in Web programming or Application development is a plus. Communication and team work skills are required to be eligible for this position. Applicants who are dedicated, open-minded and industrious could fit in more easily. There would be an entrance test about innovative-thinking skills with a view to evaluating your capability.

Benefits: This is a chance for you to enhance your soft skills and hands-on experience in the programming field. Furthermore, by participating in this project, you could expand your network and get an approach to experts in this field.
