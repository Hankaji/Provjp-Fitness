Project Description

Overview

Topic

In this project, we intend to create a fitness app equipped with A.I technology that can be the users’ private professional trainer. The app can support users in creating their own workout plan based on the statistics of users and their timetable, sharing useful medical knowledge, and diagnosing users’ disease based on their recorded data during their workout. By using A.I this will allow users to experience more efficient workout exercises and shorten the time to achieve their goals. Most importantly, this app can promote a healthier lifestyle for the users because their professional trainer can follow them anywhere, at any time and it allows people to arrange their timetable easier.

Furthermore, by developing this fitness app, we aim to raise funds for charity through the number of users who download our app; we will try to connect with charitable foundations in order to support the poor from all around Vietnam (we would mainly focus on those who live in remote areas). For example, if possible, we will host a triathlon to raise funds for free surgeries, free vaccinations for poor children and adults.

In addition, we will try to launch campaigns (it would be usually related to charitable activities but sometimes there would be prizes for users who reach their goals). There would be many online and offline challenges yearly (these challenges are also taking part in diagnosing and assessing the current status of users’ health) such as yoga competitions, holding breath challenge, balancing test, … We want to fully understand every users’ status so that our app would perform the best as it has in guiding and protecting our users.

Motivation

Our motivation for this project is promoting people to have a healthy lifestyle including both physically and mentally. This project is interesting because our app is user-friendly, relaxing, informative, … We try to focus on bringing the best experience for users when they decide to download our app by following in the pioneers’ footsteps but we will modify the whole structure with our own plan; we would refer to the previous apps/websites UI (User Interface), UX (User Experience), … with a view to harmoniously combining base systems with our up-to-date information and eye-catching design.

While doing research, we find that our app would be completely fitted in current IT trends in Vietnam since for the last 5 years, the Mobile applications development field has been chosen a lot when students graduated from universities/colleges. That’s why we believe that when our app is launched, we hope it will gain success (maybe not too much at the first phase but gradually it will have real impacts on the market and would be a real contender for related apps/websites).

Landscape

As a part of the market, we know that many applications were created before our apps such as Apple Fitness+ or Nike Run Club. Those applications provide many exercises and it is very comfortable for users to keep fit everywhere. Our application has many special features to make it different from other apps.

The first aspect is that our app can connect with smartwatches such as the Apple Watch or Galaxy Watch to collect data while the user is doing exercise and calculating calories customers have burned, as well as analyzing the collected data to identify any health issues and give advice to improve the users’ health. (in the foreseeable future there will be more compatible smartwatches and we will try to update biannually).

The second aspect is reminder, our system can notify users to take an exercise everyday and keep on a healthy track. We also provide many diet plans for users to choose. In the diet plan, we suggest healthy and diet foods, then we make exercises suitable for diet people to push their progress faster. Most importantly, our app consists of everything when it comes to healthcare’s field (Fitness, Nutrition and Medical are three most crucial sections in this area) and our app looks like an encyclopedia for healthcare’s ground.

The next aspect is we provide a bunch of exercise types such as pilates, high intensity interval training, … while the present apps on the market just help us perform running exercises. It can improve users' interest while using our apps.

Furthermore, our app can track the movement of the user, so that when the users fall, the app depends on the phone's gyroscope to decide to call an ambulance if users are in danger.

Detailed description

Aims

When we decided to pick this idea as the content for our project, our top priority aim is promoting a healthier lifestyle for anyone who is keen on keeping their body fitted. As we have mentioned, our app is equipped with an A.I that can easily recognize and follow users’ movements while they are practicing with our lessons. This feature is specifically designed for instructing users how to perform nearly as professional trainers do. Although users’ motions will not be exactly the same as trainers’ but by pointing out mistakes and showing correct forms, users will be encouraged and would likely finish their courses in no time.

In order to achieve our aim, there are some smaller goals that need to be achieved (but there are 2 main goals that we would pay more attention to); the first goal is collaborating with as many Gym & Fitness clubs as possible. We want to bring a variety of exercises, approaches by different techniques from diverse styles (such as Yoga, Boxing, Pilates, Weightlifting, Cycling, Mountain climbing, …). By this way, customers would be excited about our app and this could increase the number of healthy people.

The second goal that is also vital to our aim is the UI/UX of our app. This app is being created with a view to mostly concentrate on Millennials and generation Z (people who were born from 1981 to 1996 and from 1997 to 2012). These two generations are excellent at using smart devices and catch up with trends. In this field, we will offer users to customize their own theme, background on their devices (it would be acceptable to set our app as an open source version but only for customizing background and color themes). This feature could help any users to show off their styles or their own characteristics and it might help our customers find our app more attractive which could lead to better performances, better results and better them.

Scope and Limits

The first thing is that we want to build up an online workout platform in the COVID background in order to motivate people to keep fit while staying at home. We also connect people together by taking exercises in the same session.

The next thing we aim for is the health safety of users. Our apps can collect data from smartwatches and phones to predict falls or danger and call an ambulance if needed. Our app can base on the health index of users to calculate the best way for them to lose weight safely and more efficiently.

We design our app very friendly with users. The menu of the app is divided into many categories for each exercise type. If the users are not sure which type of exercise they need to choose, we also provide many suggestions about exercise compatible with each type of person. Exercise calendar is one of the functions of our app, it displays what exercise customers need to take in separate days, so that the users can easily manage their time. If the users are stuck in a bug, they can directly report them to us via the bug report button.

On the other hand, we have some functions that can not come true due to some limits. We can not add AI into the app because it will take a lot of time to do it while the time of assignment is limited.
